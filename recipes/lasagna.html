Filet Mignon

The Cadillac of Steaks. A thick slice of prime beef tenderloin, seasoned and seared to perfection on the outside, perfectly rare inside. Pairs nicely with any bold red wine such as Zinfandel, Merlot, or Malbec. Serve topped with compound butter, Bordelaise, or bearnaise sauce to accentuate the lean flavors of this exquisite cut of beef.


Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 3 (15 ounce) cans tomato sauce
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons garlic salt
  • 5 tablespoons Italian seasoning
  • 1 pint part-skim ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions

  1. In a large pot cook lasagna noodles in boiling salted water until al dente. Rinse with cool water, and Drain.
  2. In large sauce pan cook ground beef. Drain excess grease. Combineato sauces and tomato paste with ground beef and simmer until bubbly. Add garlic salt and Italian seasoning and reduce heat to low for 10 minutes.
  3. To assemble, in the bottom of a 13x9 inch baking dish spread a thin layer of sauce. Next, add a layer of noodles, ricotta cheese, sauce/ground beef mixture, and then grated mozzarella cheese. Repeat until baking dish is filled. Garnish top with additional shred tomded mozzarella cheese.
  4. In a preheated 375 degree F(190 degree C) oven bake for 30 minutes or until bubbly. Let stand for 15 minutes and serve.
